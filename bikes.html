Mach or Acro · Two-Wheeled Hoenn

Pick a bike,
change the map.

Rydel hands you one bike and asks you to choose. The Mach Bike is raw speed and slope-climbing — the tool of story progression. The Acro Bike is trick movement — bunny hops, wheelies, and white-rail balance — the tool of secret access. Emerald lets you swap between them at no cost, any time, and you will. Below: full capabilities, every location each bike is required, and the controls for the four field tricks Acro unlocks.

Section I

The two
bikes

One at a time, but never permanent. Rydel’s Cycles in Mauville will swap your loaner with a smile every time you ask — the closest thing in Hoenn to a free quality-of-life button.

M
No. 01 · Speed

Mach Bike

Pure speed. Doubles overworld movement and lets you climb dirt slopes that walking cannot. Speed builds over a few tiles — you must commit to a straight line before slope climbs and crack-floor jumps.

  • Climbs muddy/dirt slopes at full speed (walking is rejected)
  • Breaks cracked floors when ridden over them — falls through to the floor below
  • Outruns the Pacifidlog tide currents on Routes 132/133/134
  • Doubles your traversal speed on flat ground
Drawback

No tricks. Cannot bunny-hop, wheelie, or balance on white rails. Inside Sky Pillar this is dangerous — the Mach Bike will break the cracked floors and trap you below.

Controls

Hold a directional button to accelerate. Speed builds over ~3 tiles. Release to coast, tap perpendicular to lose all speed.

FIELD USE — Story progression, dungeon climbs, escape from Pacifidlog currents toward Sky Pillar approach.

A
No. 02 · Tricks

Acro Bike

The trick bike. Slower than Mach but unlocks bunny hops, wheelies, and white-rail traversal. The B button is your trick modifier — tap, hold, or combine with a direction.

  • Bunny Hop: tap-and-hold B with no direction — hops in place. Required for yellow Hopping Tiles.
  • Wheelie Hop: hold B then tap a direction — one-tile hop without sliding.
  • Wheelie: hold B and move — rides on the back wheel. Required for white rails AND for crossing Sky Pillar floors WITHOUT breaking them.
  • Side Hop: tap B + perpendicular direction while on a rail — jump on or off rails.
Drawback

Slower than Mach — can't outrun Pacifidlog currents, can't climb dirt slopes. Story dungeons that demand acceleration (Magma Hideout, Mt. Chimney ash) reject this bike entirely.

Controls

B is the trick modifier. Single tap = hop. Hold = wheelie. B + direction = directional hop. Practice on the empty stretch east of Mauville before committing to puzzles.

FIELD USE — Trick House late puzzles, Sky Pillar (Rayquaza), Safari Zone NE/NW expansions, Berry Master path, several Secret Bases.

The Emerald difference

Emerald is the only Gen 3 game where you can own both bikes (one at a time, free swaps) without needing to find Rydel’s missing bike like in Ruby/Sapphire. Just walk into Rydel’s in Mauville City and ask for the other one.

Section II

Rydel’s
cycles

Mauville City, central building with the bicycle on the sign. Rydel will hand you a free bike on first visit, and swap your loaner whenever you ask. No item, no cost, no cooldown.

Where

Rydel’s Cycles, central Mauville City. Two doors down from the Pokemon Center.

When

As soon as you reach Mauville — after Brawly’s Knuckle Badge in Dewford. Grab whichever bike fits the next dungeon.

Swap rules

Talk to Rydel any time to exchange bikes. No cost. Always swap before entering a long dungeon — there is no reason to suffer Sky Pillar with a Mach Bike or Magma Hideout with an Acro.

Section III

Where each bike is
required

Ten gates across Hoenn that demand a specific bike. Bring the wrong one and you walk back to Mauville.

01 · Route 112 / Jagged Pass

Mt. Chimney ash slope

Climb the soft-ash slope from Fiery Path side up toward Lavaridge approach. Walking is rejected outright.

Mach Bike
02 · Jagged Pass entrance

Magma Hideout

A series of dirt slopes inside the lava-filled cave. Cannot be navigated without Mach acceleration.

Mach Bike
03 · Route 111 desert

Mirage Tower

Cracked floors break under speed — that’s the point here. Drop through to lower floors and grab the Root or Claw Fossil.

Mach Bike
04 · Route 131 · Rayquaza

Sky Pillar

Cracked floors here will break and drop you if you ride fast. Use Acro Wheelie to traverse them slowly without breaking. Mach traps you on lower floors.

Acro Bike
05 · Trick House

Puzzle 6 — Hopping Tiles

Bunny-hop on yellow circular pads to advance through the puzzle. Walking simply ignores the tiles.

Acro Bike
06 · Route 123

Berry Master’s House

Wheelie across the white rails to reach the Berry Master and the wife who hands out rare berries for the right phrase.

Acro Bike
07 · Post-Hall of Fame

Safari Zone expansions

Hopping Tiles gate entry to the new northeast and northwest areas added after entering the Hall of Fame.

Acro Bike
08 · Route 132/133

Pacifidlog log bridges

Several connecting log bridges between houses use white rails — Acro Bike makes the village navigable instead of awkward.

Acro Bike
09 · Various

Secret Base entries

Many tree-and-rail Secret Base entry tiles require wheelie traversal. Pin a flag to a rail-tree before committing.

Acro Bike
10 · Routes 132–134

Currents toward Sky Pillar

You can’t outrun water currents on the bike, but bridge sections leading in/out reward Mach speed. Swap once you arrive.

Mach Bike
Section IV

Acro field
tricks

All four tricks are mapped to the B button alone or in combination with a direction. Practice them on the empty stretch east of Mauville — there are no consequences for missing a hop on flat ground.

Trick 01

Bunny Hop

Tap-and-hold B with no direction. Your character bounces in place. Required for the yellow Hopping Tiles in Trick House Puzzle 6 and the post-game Safari Zone expansions.

Slope slide rule

Mach Bike on a dirt slope climbs. Walking down a slope works either way; walking UP a slope is impossible without the Mach Bike. The Acro Bike will refuse the climb — if a slope blocks you, swap.

Closing transmission

Always swap before
the dungeon, not during.

Rydel is centrally located in Mauville and free. There is no reason to suffer through Sky Pillar with a Mach Bike, Magma Hideout with an Acro, or the post-game Safari with the wrong wheels. Swap, then commit.
