Chapter 05 · Off the Beaten Path

What most
trainers miss

Fifteen quiet doors in Hoenn — the random tiles, the password berries, the chambers behind Braille, the rooftop lottery that’s been printing Master Balls since 2004. Here is what the manual left out.

The Front Page

Two secrets
worth a dossier.

Most Hoenn secrets are quick footnotes. Two are full features — an island that almost never appears, and a fish that won’t evolve until you serve it the right blue cake.

No. 01 · Off Route 130

Mirage Island

1 in 65,536

A tiny island that only appears off Route 130 if any Pokemon in your party shares a Personality Value with the day’s hidden 16-bit RNG seed — roughly a one-in-sixty-five-thousand chance, rolled fresh every day. The old man in the leftmost house of Pacifidlog will tell you whether it’s there. The only things on the island are a Wynaut horde and a Liechi Berry, but it is the rarest non-event location in the cartridge.

#360 · Wynaut horde
Evolves into Wobbuffet at Lv. 15.
Field tip

Cycle as many Pokemon through your party as possible to widen the lottery. There’s no way to game it — just check daily.

No. 02 · Route 119

Feebas Tiles

water

#349 Feebas · max Beauty · #350 Milotic

Feebas only spawns on six specific tiles in Route 119, randomly selected per save. You will fish — Old Rod, Good Rod, Super Rod — on every tile in the river until one comes up Feebas. Once you find it, the location is consistent for that Trainer ID. Save it like a treasure map.

Did you know

Feebas evolves into Milotic only when its Beauty contest stat is maxed out via blue Pokeblocks — then it evolves on the next level-up. This is the only path to Milotic in vanilla Emerald.

Group I

Mirages & oddities

Things that vanish, shift, or don’t look like they should be there at all.

03 · Pacifidlog Town

Pacifidlog Tides

Currents shift directionally every few in-game hours, pushing you toward or away from Sky Pillar. Use the Mach Bike to outrun them. The morning current is the most useful for the Rayquaza route.

Hour-of-day tactic
04 · Route 134 dive

Sealed Chamber & the Regis

A Braille chamber under Route 134. Lead with Wailord, tail with Relicanth, then read the walls. Doing so unlocks Regirock, Regice, and Registeel in caves around Hoenn. The Braille is real and English-translatable.

→
Wailord · Relicanth
05 · Mauville to Verdanturf

Trainer Hill

A four-floor sequential trainer rush via the cable car. Earn record times against the default rooms; downloadable e-Reader hills (event-only) extend it. The real prize is the speedrun leaderboard.

Pack Ethers
Group II

Berry Master passwords

Tell the woman on Route 123 the right phrase and she’ll hand you a rare berry. Plant them. Grow infinite supplies.

No. 06 · Route 123

The Five Phrases

Each phrase, said exactly once per day, returns one rare berry. These berries are the basis of max-power Pokeblocks for Contests — and the only sustainable supply in vanilla Emerald.

Did you know

Each rare berry can be planted on Hoenn’s soft soil tiles, watered, and harvested for more — the only way to scale rare-berry production without trading.

No. 07 · Lilycove + others

Move Tutors of Hoenn

Several NPCs across Hoenn teach moves — some free, some paid in Battle Points, some paid in Heart Scales. The Lilycove Move Reminder takes Heart Scales for any forgotten move.

  • Slateport Battle Tent area · Counter, Mimic
  • Fallarbor Town · Mega Punch, Mega Kick equivalents
  • Sootopolis · Explosion, Self-Destruct
  • Battle Frontier · Body Slam, Double-Edge, Seismic Toss, Dream Eater (Battle Points)
  • Lilycove Move Reminder · any forgotten move for one Heart Scale
Field tip

Heart Scales drop from Rock Smash boulders and from Pickup-ability Pokemon (Linoone, Zigzagoon). Stockpile them.

Group III

Mossdeep & the space center

The eastern coast hides a Sun Stone, a rocket launch, and the only NPC in Emerald who quietly mentions Jirachi.

08 · Mossdeep, 2F

Space Center NPCs

Talk to every scientist on the second floor. Once Team Magma’s plot wraps, the man at the upper-left desk will give you the Sun Stone — the only one available pre-Frontier. Post-story, you can watch the rocket launch.

Sun Stone · rocket cutscene
09 · Mossdeep, 2F

The Jirachi Whisper

Among the space-center NPC dialogue is the only in-game reference to Jirachi’s myth. The legendary itself was distributed via Pokemon Colosseum bonus disc — the in-game text is the closest vanilla Emerald gets.

#385 Jirachi · event-only
Group IV

Lottery, slots & contests

Three places where money, coins, and Pokeblocks turn into the best TMs and Pokemon in the game.

10 · Lilycove Dept Store

Rooftop Lottery

The Pokemon Center lottery checks every Pokemon in your PC against a daily 5-digit ID. 5 matches wins a Master Ball; 4 a PP Up; 3 an Exp Share. Trade with friends to multiply your odds.

5 = Master Ball
11 · Mauville City

Game Corner Slots

Win Coins on the slots, then exchange them for TM13 Ice Beam, TM23 Iron Tail, TM24 Thunderbolt, TM30 Shadow Ball, TM35 Flamethrower. Cherry/replay strategy raises odds.

TM24 = 4000 Coins
12 · Hoenn-wide

Contest Halls

Verdanturf (Normal), Slateport (Super), Fallarbor (Hyper), Lilycove (Master). Five categories: Cool, Beauty, Cute, Smart, Tough. Win all four ranks to stamp a Pokemon Picture onto your Trainer Card.

Beauty → Milotic
13 · Route 110

The Trick House

The Trick Master rebuilds a new puzzle floor after every gym badge. Rewards include the Hard Stone, a Magnet, and three TMs the cartridge gives nowhere else: TM12 Taunt, TM31 Brick Break, TM32 Double Team.

Re-enter every badge
14 · Trick House interior

Trick Master TMs

Brick Break is essential against Battle Frontier Light Screen / Reflect spammers; Taunt shuts down setup mons; Double Team is grimy but legal. Bring Cut and Strength — many tricks hide items behind cuttable trees.

Pack HM01, HM04
Group V

Secret bases & frontier symbols

The two systems that make Emerald replayable forever — one a quiet hideout, one a brutal post-game gauntlet.

No. 15 · TM43 Secret Power

Secret Bases

Use Secret Power on certain trees, bushes, walls, and dirt mounds to claim a base. Decorate with dolls, mats, posters, and tents. Mix Records via Link Cable to import friends’ bases — their teams become NPC trainers you can battle daily for free EXP.

  • TM43 from an NPC in Slateport’s market
  • Place anywhere a Secret Power-target tile sits
  • Mix Records imports up to 10 friend bases
  • Daily-resetting NPC battles — the best EXP loop in the game
Watch out

Mix Records overwrites your local roster of bases. Visit each base before mixing if you want their items first.

No. 16 · Post-game

Battle Frontier Symbols

The artificial island past Lilycove holds seven facilities, each with a Frontier Brain. Beat each Brain to claim a Symbol. Earning all fourteen — Silver and Gold for every facility — is the true credits roll of Pokemon Emerald.
