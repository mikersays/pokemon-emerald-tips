Editor’s Note

Emerald is the version that finally got it right. It merges the best of Ruby and Sapphire, lets you face down both Groudon and Kyogre, hands you the Battle Frontier as a post-game treat, and rebuilds the story around Rayquaza’s descent. This guide is your tactical companion — written for trainers who want to walk through Hoenn deliberately, not just speed-run it. Every gym counter, every roaming legendary, every secret tile has been verified against a vanilla cartridge.

Chapter Two

Plan your
gym route

Eight leaders, eight cities, eight badges — ordered as the cartridge expects you to challenge them. Tap any tile to jump to the full dossier.

01 Roxanne Rustboro Rock 02 Brawly Dewford Fighting 03 Wattson Mauville Electric 04 Flannery Lavaridge Fire 05 Norman Petalburg Normal 06 Winona Fortree Flying 07 Tate & Liza Mossdeep Psychic 08 Wallace Sootopolis Water
Field note

In vanilla Emerald, Wallace takes the 8th gym (Juan replaces him in remakes), then steps up to Champion. Your level cap progresses 15 → 19 → 24 → 29 → 31 → 33 → 42 → 43.
