Chapter 04 · Selection Bias

The Hoenn
tier list

A working ranking of Pokemon available in vanilla Emerald, sorted by playthrough viability, movepool depth, and how cleanly each one handles the gym & Elite Four ladder. Five tiers, sixty-seven picks, every justification tied to a specific matchup.

Tier lists travel poorly. A Smogon OU ranking and a Battle Frontier streak ranking and a casual playthrough ranking all disagree on the same forty Pokemon — and they should, because the questions are different. This list answers exactly one question: given a fresh save of vanilla Emerald, which Pokemon will get you to the credits with the least friction? We weight availability against the level cap, movepool against gym typings, and time-to-payoff against your patience for grinding Bagon in Meteor Falls. Frontier viability is a tiebreaker, not the headline. Competitive ladder placement does not enter the room. Trades from FireRed/LeafGreen are excluded. Legendaries live on their own page.

S

Run-defining — trivializes major battles

7 PICKS

Swampert

water
ground

Starter (Mudkip @ Littleroot)

Bulky physical anchor; only Grass-weak. Mud Shot one-shots Wattson, Earthquake/Ice Beam erase Drake's Salamence and Flygon, and the 4x Grass weakness is a non-issue against Wallace. Solos five of eight gyms with no babying.

Sceptile

grass

Starter (Treecko @ Littleroot)

Hyper-fast Leaf Blade attacker. Base 120 Speed outpaces every E4 lead. Leaf Blade two-shots Wallace's Whiscash and Milotic; Crunch lets it pierce Tate & Liza without dying to Psychic.

Blaziken

fire
fighting

Starter (Torchic @ Littleroot)

Dual-STAB sweeper from gym 1 onward. Double Kick at Lv 16 carries Roxanne; Sky Uppercut at Lv 36 cleaves Norman's Slaking and Glacia's Walrein. Blaze Kick + Bulk Up is the cleanest Phoebe answer the starter trio offers.

Salamence

dragon
flying

Bagon @ Meteor Falls B1F (post-7th badge, Lv 25 surf area)

Pseudo-legendary 600 BST closer. 135 Attack with Dragon Claw + Earthquake + Aerial Ace + Crunch hits the entire E4 super-effectively somewhere. Intimidate softens physical hits long enough to set up a single Dragon Dance and end the run.

Metagross

steel
psychic

Beldum gift from Steven (post-game, Steven's house in Mossdeep)

Frontier-tier wall-breaker. 70/130/95 bulk eats Sidney's Dark moves while Meteor Mash two-shots them, and Earthquake dismantles Magma/Aqua leaders. Post-game only, but remains the single best Frontier mon you can build.

Manectric

electric

Electrike @ Route 110 (pre-Wattson)

Fast Electric with Intimidate utility. Available before Wattson, who he then trivializes via Quick Attack + Howl. Thunderbolt OHKOes Winona's Pelipper and Skarmory and 2HKOs Wallace's Milotic; Intimidate keeps Sharpedo and Crawdaunt out of Crunch range.

Slaking

normal

Slakoth @ Petalburg Woods (pre-Brawly)

670 BST brick that loafs every other turn. Truant is the price for a 160 Attack Earthquake that two-shots most E4 leads. Schedule Hyper Potions on the loaf turn and it functionally trades 1-for-1 with anything Glacia, Phoebe, or Sidney bring.

A

Excellent — build a team around any of these

21 PICKS

Gardevoir

psychic

Ralts @ Route 102 (4% encounter)

Calm Mind sweeper with full coverage tutors. Calm Mind + Psychic boosts past Phoebe's Dusclops, and Thunderbolt/Ice Punch tutors round it out for Wallace and Drake. Trace ability copies Levitate against Claydol in Tate & Liza.

Aggron

steel
rock

Aron @ Granite Cave B2F (pre-Brawly grind)

Physical wall with Rock Head Double-Edge. Rock Head zeroes Double-Edge recoil for spam-killing Winona's birds, and 180 base Defense soaks Drake's Dragon Claws. Earthquake patches the 4x Fighting/Ground hole for the rest of the team.

Flygon

ground
dragon

Trapinch @ Route 111 desert (Goggles required, post-Flannery)

Levitating dual-STAB pivot. Levitate gives a free switch on Wattson and Norman's Earthquake, while Dragon Claw + Earthquake + Rock Slide hits 14 of 17 types neutrally or better. Crunch from tutor cleans up Tate & Liza without tanking Psychic.

Walrein

ice
water

Spheal @ Shoal Cave (post-Winona)

Thick Fat Ice tank that walls Drake. Thick Fat halves Fire damage so Glacia's Walrein mirror-match goes your way; Surf + Ice Beam two-shots all four of Drake's dragons. Body Slam paralysis keeps Salamence in check after Intimidate fades.

Hariyama

fighting

Makuhita @ Granite Cave 1F (pre-Dewford)

Massive HP brick with Thick Fat. 144 base HP plus Thick Fat means Glacia's Ice moves chip rather than KO, and Vital Throw never misses Sidney's Cacturne. Belly Drum at full HP one-shots most non-Ghost E4 leads.

Heracross

bug
fighting

Safari Zone Northeast (post-Winona)

Megahorn nuke with Guts. Megahorn at 120 BP is the single hardest hit you can land on Phoebe's Dusclops short of a Crit. Guts + a Flame Orb turns Brick Break into a 270 BP nuke for Sidney's whole team.

Crobat

poison
flying

Zubat @ Granite Cave / Rusturf (Lv 5+)

Friendship-evo speed demon. 130 Speed outruns Winona's Altaria and Drake's Salamence. Cross Poison + Aerial Ace handles every Grass and Fighting type the game throws, and Mean Look + Fly is the standard catch tool for the Latios/Latias roamer.

Magneton

electric
steel

New Mauville (post-Wattson) — Magnemite encounter

Skarmory check and Wallace counter. 11 resistances and a Steel immunity to Poison let it wall Winona's Skarmory while Thunderbolt OHKOs it back. Two-shots every member of Wallace's gym except Whiscash, who eats a switch into Flygon.

Breloom

grass
fighting

Shroomish @ Petalburg Woods (pre-Roxanne)

Spore + Sky Uppercut priority. Spore is a 100% accuracy sleep — opens any matchup, including Norman's Slaking and Sidney's Absol. Sky Uppercut + Mach Punch + Giga Drain cover Rock, Dark, and Water without items.

Linoone

normal

Zigzagoon @ Route 101 (Lv 2, day one)

Pickup utility plus Belly Drum + ExtremeSpeed. Pickup ability nets Nuggets, Rare Candies, and Full Restores across a playthrough. Belly Drum + ExtremeSpeed (TM/breeding) is a legitimate cleanup option once the Frontier opens; otherwise it carries Cut, Surf, Strength, Rock Smash, and Dig.

Swellow

normal
flying

Taillow @ Route 104 (day one)

Guts + Facade revenge killer. Aerial Ace at Lv 13 trivializes Brawly. Held Toxic Orb + Guts + Facade is a 140 BP STAB that two-shots most of Phoebe's Banettes and the bulk of Drake's team after Intimidate-free entries.

Skarmory

steel
flying

Route 113 / 114 (post-Norman)

Spikes-laying physical wall. 140 Defense plus 9 resistances means Sidney's Mightyena and Cacturne plink for nothing while Steel Wing + Drill Peck chip back. Spikes flips Phoebe's bulky Dusclops switch-ins into 2HKOs.

Camerupt

fire
ground

Numel @ Route 112 (pre-Flannery)

Earthquake/Eruption physical-special hybrid. Solid Rock halves the 4x Water hit just enough to survive a Surf and OHKO back with Earthquake. Eruption at full HP melts Phoebe and most of Sidney; switch out before any Hydro Pump.

Tentacruel

water
poison

Surf encounters @ Route 103 onward

Bulky special Water with Liquid Ooze. Liquid Ooze punishes Sidney's Cacturne Giga Drain. Sludge Bomb + Ice Beam + Surf is a clean Drake answer, and 120 Sp.Def shrugs off Wallace's special threats.

Gyarados

water
flying

Magikarp @ Route 119 fishing (Old Rod) or Old Man trade

Intimidate + Dragon Dance cleaner. Intimidate + 125 Attack means a single Dragon Dance turns Earthquake/Bite/Surf into one-shot fodder against Drake. Magikarp grind is brutal but no other physical Water hits this hard pre-Frontier.

Donphan

ground

Phanpy @ Safari Zone (post-Mauville)

Sturdy Earthquake spammer. 120 base Attack and Defense let it eat a Thunderbolt from Wattson before flattening him with Magnitude. Rapid Spin clears Spikes for the team during Frontier streaks.

Sharpedo

water
dark

Carvanha @ Route 118 (Good Rod, post-Norman)

Glass-cannon Crunch + Surf. Rough Skin chips contact attackers and 95 Speed lets it Crunch Tate & Liza's Lunatone before Calm Mind ever lands. Frail enough to fold to a stiff breeze, but it earns the slot in any rush comp.

Kingdra

water
dragon

Horsea @ Route 132 surf (post-Wallace)

Drake-counter with no 4x weakness. The only Dragon in Hoenn with no 4x weakness — Ice does neutral damage. Hydro Pump + Dragon Breath is a clean Drake mirror, and Sleep Talk gives a Frontier-grade safety net.

Glalie

ice

Snorunt @ Shoal Cave inner ice room (post-Winona)

Self-Destruct closer with Ice Beam. Inner Focus blocks Hypnosis from Tate & Liza's Lunatone. Ice Beam two-shots Drake; Self-Destruct from a Choice-less attacker still cleanly removes any single Phoebe/Glacia ace.

Cradily

rock
grass

Lileep fossil @ Mirage Tower (Claw Fossil pick) — one-time

Suction Cup wall with Ingrain + Giga Drain. Six resistances including Wallace's STAB Water (with Recover support via Ingrain). Giga Drain + Ancient Power covers Wallace, Glacia's Sealeo, and Drake's Flygon in one moveset.

Armaldo

rock
bug

Anorith fossil @ Mirage Tower (Root Fossil pick) — one-time

Battle Armor offensive fossil. Battle Armor blocks crits from Sidney's Slash spam and Phoebe's Banette Pursuit. Rock Slide + Brick Break + Ancient Power is one of the cleanest Sidney sweeps available pre-Frontier.

B

Solid contributors — niche or compromised

22 PICKS

Ludicolo

water
grass

Lotad @ Route 102 (4% encounter); needs Water Stone

Rain Dish stallbreaker. After the Water Stone (sold at Mossdeep), Ludicolo only fears Bug/Flying/Poison. Giga Drain + Ice Beam handles Wallace and Drake; Leech Seed + Substitute is a legitimate Frontier set.

Shiftry

grass
dark

Seedot @ Route 102 (4% encounter); needs Leaf Stone

Sun-Day-into-Solar Beam abuser. Early Bullet Seed + Faint Attack covers gym 1 and is the only mid-tier Dark with Explosion. Falls off without a Leaf Stone (purchasable post-Wallace) and slow Speed limits Frontier viability.

Pelipper

water
flying

Wingull @ Route 104 (day one)

Cheap early Surfer with Roost-tier longevity. Carries Surf and Fly at the same time, freeing two team slots. Ice Beam + Surf + Fly is enough to limp through Wallace and Drake's Flygon, but offensive stats are mediocre.

Roselia

grass
poison

Roselia @ Route 117 (post-Wattson)

Special tank with Toxic + Giga Drain. Natural Cure clears burns/sleep on switch. Synthesis + Giga Drain self-heals through Wallace's Whiscash, but stays Roselia in Gen 3 (Roserade requires later-gen Shiny Stone).

Xatu

psychic
flying

Natu @ Safari Zone (post-Mauville)

Synchronize utility breeder. Synchronize transfers natures for breeding, and Psychic + Steel Wing covers most B-tier matchups. Outclassed offensively by Gardevoir but fills the early Flying-Psychic dual-coverage slot.

Claydol

ground
psychic

Baltoy @ Route 111 desert (Goggles required)

Levitate wall with Earthquake. Immune to Wattson and Norman's Earthquake spam. Rapid Spin + Light Screen + Reflect makes it a Frontier doubles staple, but mediocre 70/70 offenses cap its solo carry.

Whiscash

water
ground

Barboach @ Route 114 fishing (Good Rod)

Earthquake + Surf with Spark immunity. Same dual STAB as Swampert with Amnesia for boosting against Wallace. Quad Grass weakness and lower bulk relegate it to B; works well as a Swampert understudy if your starter was Treecko or Torchic.

Crawdaunt

water
dark

Corphish @ Route 102 fishing (Old Rod, post-Wattson)

Hyper-Cutter + Crabhammer-style raw power. Hyper Cutter blocks Intimidate, so it goes toe-to-toe with Sharpedo. Slow Speed (55) is the killer — it always trades, never sweeps.

Banette

ghost

Shuppet @ Mt. Pyre (post-Winona)

Insomnia Shadow Ball striker. Insomnia blocks Lunatone's Hypnosis at Tate & Liza. Will-O-Wisp + Shadow Ball cripples physical attackers, but 64 Speed and zero recovery keep it out of A.

Dusclops

ghost

Duskull @ Mt. Pyre (post-Winona)

Pure wall with Will-O-Wisp + Pain Split. 135/135 Defense/SpDef. Will-O-Wisp + Pain Split is the textbook Frontier stall set. No reliable recovery and pitiful offense — a true wall, nothing more.

Plusle

electric

Route 110 (pre-Wattson)

Doubles support specialist. Helping Hand + Encore + Thunder Wave is the cleanest doubles assist Hoenn offers, especially for Tate & Liza prep. Solo offense is too low for singles main story.

Minun

electric

Route 110 (pre-Wattson)

Doubles support specialist (mirror of Plusle). Higher base SpDef than Plusle; identical role. Pair with a heavy hitter in the Battle Tent doubles for free wins.

Volbeat

bug

Route 117 grass (male only)

Tail Glow + Baton Pass setup mon. Tail Glow is a +3 Sp.Atk boost — Baton Pass it to Manectric or Gardevoir for an instant sweep. Gimmick, but the only legal Tail Glow user pre-Frontier.

Illumise

bug

Route 117 grass (female only)

Charm + Encore setup mon (Volbeat partner). Encore traps setup moves (looking at you, Bulk Up Slaking). Pairs with Volbeat for the only legal +3 SpAtk Baton Pass chain in Hoenn.

Beautifly

bug
flying

Wurmple @ Route 101 (50/50 evo into Silcoon)

Silver Wind RNG-boost specialist. Silver Wind has a 10% chance to boost every stat — practical setup over a 5-turn Frontier streak. Acceptable Brawly answer, irrelevant by mid-game.

Dustox

bug
poison

Wurmple @ Route 101 (50/50 evo into Cascoon)

Toxic + Light Screen staller. Shield Dust ignores secondary effects. Toxic + Light Screen + Whirlwind shuffles set-up sweepers, but offense is nonexistent.

Spinda

normal

Route 113 (post-Mauville)

Confusion-spam gimmick mon. Own Tempo + Teeter Dance flips a doubles match upside-down. Each Spinda has a unique sprite pattern; offensive ceiling is low but the Frontier doubles utility is real.

Chimecho

psychic

Mt. Pyre summit grass (~1% encounter)

Bulky Psychic with Heal Bell. Heal Bell + Psychic + Calm Mind makes it the one Frontier cleric you can catch. Encounter rate is the only reason it's not A-tier; bring Sweet Scent and a Repel.

Mawile

steel

Granite Cave / Victory Road (rare)

Intimidate trickster with Iron Defense. Intimidate + Iron Defense + Baton Pass is a Frontier doubles staple. Iron Head from move tutor lands flinches, but 50/85 offenses cap it solo.

Lunatone

rock
psychic

Meteor Falls (Sapphire/Emerald exclusive)

Levitate special wall. Calm Mind + Psychic + Ice Beam handles Drake's Flygon and Salamence in one set. Available right after Brawly and stays serviceable through E4 if leveled.

Solrock

rock
psychic

Meteor Falls (Ruby/Emerald exclusive)

Sunny Day SolarBeam + Levitate. Levitate + four resistances. SolarBeam under sun handles Wallace's Whiscash; underwhelming Speed and weak Special movepool keep it out of A.

Sableye

dark
ghost

Granite Cave B1F (Sapphire/Emerald), Smeltery / Rusturf for Ruby trade — natively in Emerald

Two type-immunities (Normal, Psychic, Fighting). Pre-Fairy era, Dark/Ghost has only one weakness: nothing. Recover + Knock Off + Shadow Ball is a Frontier stall package; low stats limit single-mon dominance.

C

Workable but slow — bench-tier or transitional

11 PICKS

Cacturne

grass
dark

Cacnea @ Route 111 desert (Goggles)

Spikes + Needle Arm flincher. Sand Veil in the desert turns it into an evasion menace. Spikes + Needle Arm + Faint Attack is workable, but 55 Speed and middling bulk make it a B-minus pick.

Vibrava

ground
dragon

Trapinch evo (Lv 35) — pre-Flygon stage

Mid-evo with Levitate, no real STAB until Lv 45. Decent enough to hold the slot until Lv 45 Flygon. Don't fight Drake with Vibrava — wait the ten levels, the spike is real.

Wailord

water

Wailmer @ Route 129 surf (post-Wallace)

Bulkiest HP in Gen 3. 170 HP soaks Drake's Dragon Claws long enough to set up Water Spout. Outclassed by Walrein and Sharpedo for offensive Water duty, but matchless as a Frontier mixed wall.

Castform

normal

Gift from Weather Institute (post-Wattson)

Forecast weather-form gimmick. Changes type with weather. Useful in doubles with a Sunny Day partner; outright bad in singles. Take it for the Pokedex slot.

Kecleon

normal

Route 119 invisible static encounter (Devon Scope)

Color Change brawler. Color Change resists the move it just took, so Sidney's Knock Off + Slash combo never lands a super-effective hit. Slow as molasses; pure novelty pick.

Absol

dark

Route 120 (rare)

Crit-stacked physical Dark. Super Luck + Razor Claw (Frontier prize) push crit rate to 50%. Swords Dance + Aerial Ace one-shots Phoebe's whole team; frailness keeps it from A.

Zangoose

normal

Route 114 grass (Emerald exclusive)

Immunity Toxic Orb Facade attacker. Immunity prevents poison, so Toxic Orb skips it — Facade alone needs another item. Slash + Crush Claw is a passable physical Sidney check, but lacks a true 6th-slot win condition.

Seviper

poison

Route 114 grass (Sapphire exclusive — trade-only in Emerald)

Poison Tail / Glare paralysis support. Glare paralyzes Ground types and Crunch handles Phoebe. Trade-locked in Emerald; bring it only if you want type variety.

Beldum

steel
psychic

Steven gift (Mossdeep, post-game)

Patience-tax mon — only knows Take Down + Hold Back until Lv 20. Pre-Metang stage is terrible. Listed only as a bridge note: grind it to Lv 45 and it becomes the S-tier Metagross — until then, bench it.

Bagon

dragon

Meteor Falls B1F (post-7th badge)

Pre-Salamence grind — slow until Lv 50. Same trap as Beldum: a 600-BST payoff at Lv 50. Grind in Meteor Falls Lv 35-40 wild battles or bring an Exp Share.

Numel

fire
ground

Route 112 (pre-Flannery)

Pre-Camerupt stage with abysmal Speed. Listed because the Numel-to-Camerupt jump at Lv 33 is enormous; treat the pre-evo as a setup phase rather than a battler.

HM

Honorable mentions — situational standouts & bridge-stage notes

6 PICKS

Altaria

dragon
flying

Swablu @ Route 114 (post-4th badge); evolves Lv 35

Bulky Dragon Dance pivot. Sing + Dragon Dance + Earthquake + Aerial Ace is a fine alternative to Salamence if you want a Hoenn-native Dragon ten levels earlier. Loses out only because Salamence outclasses it after Lv 50.

Milotic

water

Feebas @ Route 119 specific tiles (or Beauty contest evo)

Marvel Scale + Recover special wall. Marvel Scale boosts Defense by 1.5x when statused. Recover + Ice Beam + Surf + Toxic is a true Frontier-tier set. Listed in HM only because the catch process is brutal — six tiles on Route 119 with a 50% encounter rate.

Lairon

steel
rock

Aron evo @ Lv 32 — Granite Cave / Iron Island

Bridge-stage tank. Strong enough to anchor against Norman and Winona before the Lv 42 Aggron evolution. Use as a holdover — don't expect more.

Vigoroth

normal

Slakoth evo @ Lv 18 — Petalburg Woods

Vital Spirit, no Truant penalty. Hyper-active intermediate before Slaking. Faint Attack + Slash + Bulk Up keeps it real until Lv 36 Slaking — at which point Truant returns and you swap roles.

Wynaut

psychic

Egg gift @ Lavaridge old woman (post-Flannery)

Counter / Mirror Coat trap mon. Shadow Tag + Counter/Mirror Coat is a Frontier favourite for trapping single-target attackers. Singles main story role is essentially zero.

Tropius

grass
flying

Route 119 (rare)

Listed again here as a 4-of-a-kind HM slave (Cut/Fly/Strength/Flash). The only Pokemon in Hoenn that can legally hold Cut, Fly, Strength, AND Flash simultaneously. Frees four party slots.

Method note

Rankings prioritize main-story efficiency: how cleanly each Pokemon handles Hoenn's eight gyms, the rivals, the Magma/Aqua plot, and the Sidney/Phoebe/Glacia/Drake/Wallace E4 ladder. We weight (1) availability timing relative to level cap, (2) movepool depth without TM hoarding, (3) gym/E4 matchup coverage, and (4) time-investment-to-payoff. We DO NOT rank by competitive Smogon ladder performance — Frontier viability is a tiebreaker, not the headline.

Three rosters

Pre-built six-Pokemon teams

If you want to skip the deliberation, lift one of these rosters wholesale. Each is internally coherent — coverage, role distribution, and gym matchups are accounted for.

Pure Pressure

Six attackers, no fillers. Built to sweep Hoenn before anyone gets to move twice.

  • Blaziken Lead — Sky Uppercut + Blaze Kick
  • Sceptile Speed sweeper — Leaf Blade + Crunch
  • Salamence Closer — Dragon Dance + Earthquake
  • Manectric Wattson/Winona/Wallace — Thunderbolt
  • Sharpedo Crunch revenge killer for Tate & Liza
  • Heracross Phoebe/Sidney nuke — Megahorn + Brick Break
All-Weather Field Team

Two attackers, two pivots, one wall, one cleric. The textbook playthrough roster.

  • Swampert Anchor — Earthquake + Ice Beam + Surf
  • Gardevoir Calm Mind + Psychic + Thunderbolt
  • Flygon Pivot — Levitate + Dragon Claw
  • Aggron Wall — Rock Head Double-Edge
  • Crobat Speed control + Mean Look + Cross Poison
  • Linoone HM slave + Pickup utility
The Long Stall

Walls, Recover, Toxic, switch. Made for Frontier streaks; main story works fine too.

  • Walrein Thick Fat Ice tank — Surf + Ice Beam + Body Slam
  • Skarmory Spikes + Roar + Steel Wing
  • Dusclops Will-O-Wisp + Pain Split + Shadow Ball
  • Milotic Marvel Scale + Recover + Toxic + Ice Beam
  • Cradily Suction Cups + Ingrain + Giga Drain
  • Magneton Sturdy + Thunderbolt — Wallace/Skarmory check
