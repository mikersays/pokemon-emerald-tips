Chapter 04 · Selection Bias

The Hoenn
tier list

A working ranking of Pokemon available in vanilla Emerald, sorted by playthrough viability, movepool depth, and raw ceiling. S is broken; A is excellent; B is solid; honorable mentions are situational standouts.

S

Run-defining

5 PICKS

Swampert

water
ground

Best starter. Only one weakness (4× Grass). Incredible bulk and movepool. Solos most gyms.

Salamence

dragon
flying

Pseudo-legendary, 600 BST. Bagon caught in Meteor Falls (post-7th). Late but world-ending.

Metagross

steel
psychic

Pseudo-legendary; Beldum is a gift from Steven post-game. Insane bulk; Meteor Mash sweeps Frontier.

Blaziken

fire
fighting

Best dual-STAB starter. Sky Uppercut + Blaze Kick covers most of Hoenn.

Manectric

electric

Lightning Rod ability, fast, learns Thunderbolt/Thunder. Solos Winona and Wallace.

A

Excellent — build a team around any of these

9 PICKS

Breloom

grass
fighting

Mach Punch priority, Effect Spore (status on contact), Sky Uppercut. Beats Roxanne, Norman, Sidney solo.

Sceptile

grass

120 base Speed. Leaf Blade is the best physical Grass move in the game.

Flygon

ground
dragon

Trapinch in the desert → Vibrava (Lv 35) → Flygon (Lv 45). Levitate immunity to Ground.

Aggron

steel
rock

Sturdy/Rock Head. Spam Double-Edge with no recoil. Massive Defense.

Gardevoir

psychic

Ralts on Route 102 is rare but evolves at Lv 20 and Lv 30. Calm Mind + Psychic + T-Bolt + Ice Punch is end-game tier.

Hariyama

fighting

Massive HP, Thick Fat, Vital Throw + Brick Break. Hard counter to Sidney and Glacia.

Swellow

normal
flying

Taillow caught Route 104 day one. Guts + Toxic Orb + Facade is a Frontier staple.

Crobat

poison
flying

Zubat → Golbat → Crobat (high friendship). Mean Look catches roaming Latios/Latias.

Milotic

water

Marvel Scale + Recover + Ice Beam. Tedious to evolve (Feebas / Beauty contest stat) but elite.

B

Solid contributors

6 PICKS

Walrein

ice
water

Spheal in Shoal Cave → Sealeo (Lv 32) → Walrein (Lv 44). Thick Fat Ice attacker that survives Fire moves.

Camerupt

fire
ground

Numel from Route 112. Magnitude + Flamethrower covers a lot — but 4× Water weak.

Linoone

normal

Belly Drum + Extreme Speed + Pickup ability for free items. Best HM slave with Cut/Surf/Strength/Dig support.

Absol

dark

Route 120 (rare). Super Luck + Slash for crit fishing; Swords Dance + Aerial Ace devastates Phoebe.

Tentacruel

water
poison

Liquid Ooze prevents drain heals against you. Sludge Bomb + Surf + Ice Beam coverage.

Magneton

electric
steel

Sturdy/Magnet Pull, traps Steels. Magneton-locked in Emerald (Magnezone is a later-gen evo).

HM

Honorable mentions — situational standouts

3 PICKS

Ludicolo

water
grass

Lotad on Route 102 (rare). Rain Dish + Swift Swim. Unique typing — only weak to Bug/Flying/Poison.

Slaking

normal

Insane stats but Truant halves uptime. Only viable in Frontier with Skill Swap support.

Altaria

dragon
flying

Swablu evolves at Lv 35. Bulky Dragon with Dragon Dance + Earthquake.

Method note

Rankings consider availability timing (when you can catch it), evolution speed, movepool, type matchups across Hoenn’s gyms and Elite Four, and Battle Frontier ceiling. Trades from FireRed/LeafGreen are excluded. Legendaries are ranked separately on the Legendaries page.
